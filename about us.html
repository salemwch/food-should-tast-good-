Our Story

HOW WE GOT STARTED

I love food. I’ve been working in restaurants and grocery stores my whole life, and if I’ve learned anything, it’s that food tastes best when it’s made with real ingredients. That’s why I started my own company, dedicated to making wholesome snacks. As for the name, Food Should Taste Good™, it kind of wrote itself.

Pete Lescoe

FOUNDER Our Story Non-GMO Commitment Founder Pete Lescoe created Food Should Taste Good, Inc in 2006 with the goal of making a unique new snack with great taste, real ingredients, and sophisticated flavor - the qualities he is most passionate about in food. With a name that speaks for itself, Food Should Taste Good™ chips are made from high quality ingredients. All chip varieties are gluten free, cholesterol free, have zero grams trans fats. They are also certified Kosher. Plus, many varieties are certified vegan. Food Should Taste Good™ chips are available nationally at conventional grocers and natural food markets.
